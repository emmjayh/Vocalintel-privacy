    <h2>On-Device AI Processing</h2>
    <p>Vocal Intel uses on-device AI models (Parakeet CTC for speech recognition, Qwen3.5 for analysis) that run entirely on your phone. These models:</p>
    <ul>
        <li>Do not require an internet connection</li>
        <li>Do not send data to any external service</li>
        <li>Process your audio locally and discard it after transcription unless you choose to save it</li>
    </ul>

    <h2>Third-Party Services</h2>
    <p>Vocal Intel does not integrate with any third-party analytics, advertising, crash reporting, or data collection services. There are no SDKs in the app that transmit data externally.</p>

    <p>Vocal Intel does not knowingly collect data from anyone, including children under the age of 13. As the app collects no data whatsoever, it presents no privacy risk to users of any age.</p>
